Somesh Sahu

Somesh Sahu (born in Odisha, India) is an Indian learner, technology enthusiast, and aspiring author. He holds a diploma in Electrical Engineering from Adarsha College of Engineering and a Post Graduate Diploma in Computer Applications from ITCT Angul.

Sahu began his professional journey as a Data Verifier at the District Court Digitization Hub (DCDH) in Angul. In this role, he worked on categorizing case types, labeling documents, and improving record-keeping accuracy. His time there gave him experience in managing information, paying attention to details, and streamlining data-entry processes with self-developed tools.

After this, he shifted his focus fully to learning and upskilling. He is currently studying frontend development, including HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, with the aim of building practical, user-friendly web applications. Alongside frontend, he explores Python programming for data handling and automation. He also experiments with organizing systems, using tools like **Obsidian** to design personal workflows for journaling, note-taking, and project management.

Sahu’s interests extend to writing and creative work. He is working on his first book, Hence Proved, which collects his reflections on life lessons and personal growth.

He has expressed a long-term goal of combining his technical skills with storytelling and creativity. By doing so, Sahu hopes to move toward projects that are both practical in technology and meaningful in expression, reflecting his dual interest in structured systems and human experiences.
